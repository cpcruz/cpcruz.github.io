Holy Moly Guacamole

Ingredients

  • Avocados, peeled, pitted - 2
  • Red Onion, chopped - 2
  • Jalapeno Pepper, minced - 1/2 cup
  • Cucumber, chopped - 1
  • Tomato, diced - 1
  • Lemon Juice - 1 tsp
  • Corn - 1/2 cup
  • Salt and Pepper - to taste
  • Chopped Fresh Cilantro Leaves - to taste
  • Tortilla Chips

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, mash the avocados, leaving them as chunky or smooth as desired.
  2. Mix in the onion, jalapeno, cucumber, tomato, corn, salt, pepper, lemon juice, and cilantro.
  3. Serve immediately with tortilla chips.
